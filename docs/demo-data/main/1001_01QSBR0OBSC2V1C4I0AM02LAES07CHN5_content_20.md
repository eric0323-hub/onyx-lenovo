### Symptom

A limited number of Yoga C740-14 systems manufactured prior to 12/1/2019 may experience an issue with the LCD Hinge separating from the LCD Cover. This can be seen in the lower corners of the LCD assembly, or along the bottom edge near the hinge.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT509665/Yoga C740_20191203152243972.jpg) ![Rear view](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT509665/C740 Hinge Rear view_20201020141021908.jpg)

### Applicable Brands

Yoga

### Applicable Systems

Yoga C740-14 with a manufacturing date prior to 12/1/2019.

### Solution

Check systems for signs of customer induced damage. If no evidence is seen, replace the LCD cover.

For removal and installation instructions, refer to the HMM or Servicer training videos at the following links.

**[Hardware Maintenance Manual - Yoga C740-14IML, Yoga C740-15IML](http://download.lenovo.com/consumer/mobiles_pub/c740_14iml_15iml_hmm_201908.pdf)** **[Removal and Replacement Videos - Yoga C740-14 (81TC)](https://support.lenovo.com/us/en/solutions/HT509200)**
