### Symptom

System hangs after sleep.

### Applicable Systems

Yoga book (Lenovo YB1-X91)

### Operating Systems

Windows 10 64-bit

### System Is Configured With

Wi-Fi driver lower than 1.558.53.14

### Solution

Download the Wi-Fi driver from [here](http://support.lenovo.com/downloads/DS119180) and update to 1.558.53.14 or later.

### Workaround

Press the power key for 12 seconds to power it on.
