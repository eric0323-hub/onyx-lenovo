### Description

The fingerprint reader may stop working and is not recognized in Device Manager.

### Applicable Brands

ThinkPad

### Applicable Systems

X1 Yoga 3rd Gen (Type 20LD, 20LE, 20LF, 20LG)

### Solution

1. Try replacing the fingerprint reader cable before trying other fixes. This cable can be accessed by removing the internal battery.  
      
    ![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT509946/rsz_fpr_replacement_20200212210037380.png)![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT509946/rsz_fpr_20200212210233532.jpg)

2. Replace with an improved fingerprint reader cable (with mylar) by ordering one of the following FRUs for X1 Yoga 3rd Gen based on requirement
   1. FRU P/N- **5C11A10948** (FPR cable- 10pcs)
   2. FRU P/N- **01AY966** (Base Misc kit contains FPR cable- 1pc)
