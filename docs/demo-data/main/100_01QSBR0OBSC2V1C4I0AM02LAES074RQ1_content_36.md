### Symptom

The built-in display flickers after resuming from sleep or hibernation during video playback.

Scenario 1:

1. Play a video file.
2. The device goes into sleep or hibernation.
3. Power on the device.
4. Notice that the top half of the built-in display flickers to solid black.

Scenario 2:

1. Attach an external monitor to the device.
2. Play a video file.
3. The device goes into hibernation.
4. Power on the device.
5. Notice that the top half of the built-in display flickers to solid black.

### Applicable Brands

ThinkPad

### Applicable Systems

ThinkPad X1 Fold Gen 1 (Machine Types: 20RK, 20RL)

### Operating Systems

Windows 10 64-bit

### Solution

Restart the device.

### Workaround

1. Try to switch the playback window to full screen if it is not in full screen before the device goes into sleep or hibernation.
2. Close the playback window before the device goes into sleep or hibernation.
