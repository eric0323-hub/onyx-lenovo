### Symptom

Systems manufactured prior to April 17, 2025 may exhibit symptom of no display.

The flex printed cable (FPC) may be unseated from systemboard connector.

![connector](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT517923/Connector_20250718021538144.jpg)

### Applicable Brands

Chromebook

### Applicable Systems

Lenovo Chromebook Duet EDU G2  (Type: 83HK, 83HJ, 83HH)

### Solution

Reseat connector and order FRU: **5T11T60924**(Gold Mylar)

* Remove LCD and flip 180, make sure to unstick the FPC from the battery completely.

![FPC1](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT517923/FPC_cable_20250718022152356.png)

* Locate the adhesive patch on the FPC cable.

![FPC2](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT517923/FPC_cable2_20250718022653411.png)

* Apply FRU: **5T11T60924**(Gold Mylar) to completely cover the adhesive patch. The smooth non-stick side should be facing the battery

.![FPC3](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT517923/FPC_cable3_20250718023302222.png)
