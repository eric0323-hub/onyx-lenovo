### Symptom

Systems may demonstrate random keys not working or intermittently working.

This is caused by damage to the keyboard ribbon cable.

*![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Keyboard Cable Damage_20190313134116107.jpg)*

### Affected Brands

ideapad

### Affected Systems

* ideapad 130-15IKB
* ideapad 130-15AST

### System Is Configured With

Keyboard manufactured before Sep of 2018.

### Solution

Reroute the cable as shown below.

Step 1: Remove the Screws securing the Fan and disconnect the fan cable.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step1_20190319201617740.jpg)

Step 2: Remove the Fan from the machine.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step 2_20190319201727549.jpg)

Step 3: Disconnect the Keyboard Cable.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step 3_20190319201800111.jpg)

Step 4: Gently bend the Keyboard Cable into a U shape and slowly insert it into the gap between the cover and motherboard.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step 4_20190319202047562.jpg)

NOTE: DO NOT FOLD THE KEYBOARD CABLE FLAT!

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step 5 Note_20190319202255577.jpg)

Step 5: Insert the Cable into the gap until the white line aligns with the edge of the metal cover.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step 6_20190319202430340.jpg)

Step 6: Insert the Keyboard Cable into the connector on the Motherboard.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step 7_20190319202519233.jpg)

Step 7: Reinstall the Fan assembly.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step 8_2019031920275659.jpg)

NOTE: AFTER RE-ROUTING THE CABLE IT CAN'T GO OVER THE FAN HOUSING! IF IT DOES REPEAT STEPS 5 THRU 7!

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508055/Step 9_20190319203720239.jpg)
