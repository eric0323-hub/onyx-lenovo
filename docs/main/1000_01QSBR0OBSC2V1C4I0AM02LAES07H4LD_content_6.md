## Symptom

The touchpad on Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 5 Touchpad 14Q8X9 intermittently stops working, fails to detect, or responds slowly.

## Applicable Systems

Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 5 14Q8X9

## Solution

Assign Labor only to call and fix the issue without parts. Touchpad pull pad half cut and re-fixing will resolve the issue.

![](https://ukm.lenovo.com/prod-api/file/download?key=accessory/efff5b547bde4b83a29dbcea4b5d5dcf-1764663698058.jpg&name=Screenshot 2025-12-02 155123..jpg)

Please follow the steps below:

1. Disconnect the touchpad FFC cable and cut the blue pull pad tape’s extra length.

![](https://ukm.lenovo.com/prod-api/file/download?key=accessory/9de39a5ac24c457f92325da35c6002e5-1764663833904.jpg&name=Screenshot 2025-12-02 155210..jpg)![](https://ukm.lenovo.com/prod-api/file/download?key=accessory/af6e4de4e211489487cbf1794979fd37-1764663856366.jpg&name=Screenshot 2025-12-02 155231..jpg)![](https://ukm.lenovo.com/prod-api/file/download?key=accessory/c4ada48c46c94609bf179f6c071e5fbc-1764663865240.jpg&name=Screenshot 2025-12-02 155253..jpg)

2. Remove the black mylar tape pasted on top of the touchpad. (as shown in the snap below).

![](https://ukm.lenovo.com/prod-api/file/download?key=accessory/80b4ccaa9b5248ea9314bc4aabbc346d-1764663637782.jpg&name=Screenshot 2025-12-02 155424..jpg)

3. Fix the touchpad cable and apply the black Mylar above the touchpad cable.

![](https://ukm.lenovo.com/prod-api/file/download?key=accessory/aaf353003bd241f6bca9f1e49a7979b6-1764663464916.jpg&name=Screenshot 2025-12-02 155440..jpg)![](https://ukm.lenovo.com/prod-api/file/download?key=accessory/67907c1bd3c84715b8fbe8ccebdff340-1764663320847.jpg&name=Screenshot 2025-12-02 155502..jpg)
