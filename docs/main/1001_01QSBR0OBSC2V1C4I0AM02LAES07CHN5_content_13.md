### Symptom

Attached keyboard does not respond to user input when pressed.

### Applicable Brands

Lenovo

### Applicable Systems

Lenovo Tablet 10

### System Is Configured With

Keyboard dock build date is before October 2018.

### Solution

Replace the keyboard with a known good keyboard. If this does not resolve the issue, replace the system board.

**Note:** If the keyboard does not work, do not use (or test) this keyboard on other working Tablet 10 units, as the faulty keyboard may induce damage on the system side.

### Additional Information

The issue may be caused by a known Manufacturing cable pinch issue, in keyboards built before October 2018. The failure rate is low, approximately 0.2% (1 out of 500).
