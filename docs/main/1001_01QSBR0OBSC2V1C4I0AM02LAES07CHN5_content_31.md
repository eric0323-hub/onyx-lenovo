## Symptom

The Yoga Book 9 Bluetooth keyboard has a relatively short continuous usage time of approximately two hours.

## Applicable Systems

Yoga Book 9 14IAH10 (Type: 83KJ)

## Solution

In brightly lit environments, turning off the keyboard backlight can extend usage time and improve experience.

For extended use with the backlight on in dimly lit environments, connect the keyboard to a USB-C power source.
