### Symptom

Some keys may malfunction or not work properly.

### Affected Brands

Chromebook

### Affected Systems

300e Chromebook (MT: 81H0)

### System Is Configured With

Chrome OS

### Operating Systems

Chrome OS

### Solution

When replacing the 300e Chromebook keyboard. Ensure the keyboard ribbon cable is not folded or bent.

Step 1 - Identify the keyboard ribbon cable is only bent in the areas marked below. If the cable is bent in an area not highlighted, straighten and smooth out cable.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508078/2019-03-20_8-44-00_20190320124517814.jpg)

Step 2 - Check keyboard ribbon cable is bent in the correct direction in the areas shown below.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508078/2019-03-20_8-58-07_20190320130147622.jpg)

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508078/2019-03-20_9-04-18_20190320133507817.jpg)

Step 3 - Keyboard and Trackpad cables should be installed horizontally, not skewed.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508078/2019-03-20_8-47-38_20190320134852748.jpg)

Step 4 - During reassemble, press in this area of the keyboard ribbon cable to prevent the cable from bending incorrectly.

![](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508078/2019-03-20_9-07-40_20190320130840755.jpg)
