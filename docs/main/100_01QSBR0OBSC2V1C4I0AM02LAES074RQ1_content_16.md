### Description

This article provides information about the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for Yoga 9i 2-in-1 Aura Edition (14", 10).

### Windows System

#### How to update or install the latest device driver?

Update and install the latest driver using one of the following methods:

* From the Lenovo Support Web site:  
  
  1. Go to <https://pcsupport.lenovo.com> and type the product name or the serial number of your computer into the search box and select your product from the drop-down list.  
  
  2. Click **Drivers & Software**. Click **Select Drivers** under the **Manual Update** tab. Select and download the update package you want.  
  
  3. Follow the instruction at <https://support.lenovo.com/solutions/HT074189> to manually install the driver.
* From Windows Update:  
  
  1. Type **Windows Update** in the Windows search box and press Enter.  
  
  2. Click **Check for Updates**. If an update package is available, it will be automatically downloaded and installed.
* From the Lenovo Vantage app:  
  
  1. Open Lenovo Vantage. Click **Menu** icon > **System Update** > **CHECK FOR UPDATES**.  
  
  2. If an update package is available, follow the on-screen instructions to download and install the package.

For more information, refer to [Popular Topics: Drivers, Drivers Update](http://support.lenovo.com/solutions/HT504759).



#### How to troubleshoot blue screen errors?

Blue screen errors can occur if a serious problem causes Windows to shut down or restart unexpectedly.

You can try the Blue Screen Troubleshooter in the Get Help app.

1. Type **Get Help** in the Windows search box and then press Enter.
2. In the search box of the Get Help app, type **Troubleshoot BSOD error**.
3. Follow the guided walkthrough.


#### How to reset or restore Windows?

* Use Lenovo recovery options.  
  
  1. Go to <https://support.lenovo.com/HowToCreateLenovoRecovery>.  
  
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions.
* Use Windows recovery options.  
  
  1. Go to <https://pcsupport.lenovo.com>.  
  
  2. Detect your computer or manually select your computer model.  
  
  3. Click **Diagnostics** > **Operating System Diagnostics** and then follow the on-screen instructions.

#### What should I do if my Lenovo Yoga Pen is not detected or does not work?

If your pen is not detected or does not work, try the following solutions one by one to troubleshoot and fix the issue:​

1. Charge the pen.  
   **Note:** The charging time might vary depending on pen types.
2. Establish the Bluetooth connection between your pen and your PC properly. Use the following steps:  
   
   a. Click the quick settings area ![Quick settings area](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT517335/Quick settings area_20250211090929278.jpg) on the right side of the taskbar.  
   
   b. Click the **Bluetooth** settings and turn on Bluetooth.  
   
   c. Select the pen in the list and follow on-screen instructions.
3. Update **Lenovo Pen Settings**, an app preinstalled on your PC. Make sure that your pen has paired to your PC through Bluetooth.


#### Why does my computer start automatically when opening the lid?

Your computer may have **Flip to Start** enabled. Many Lenovo notebook computers include a sensor that can detect the angle at which the lid is opened. When you open the lid, the sensor can detect this behavior. If **Flip to Start** is enabled, the computer will respond by starting up automatically.

If you don't like this feature, you can disable it. **Flip to Start** can be enabled and disabled in:

* [Lenovo Vantage](http://support.lenovo.com/solutions/ht505081)
* Firmware Setup Utility

For Lenovo Vantage: Open the Vantage app, click **Menu**, **Device Settings**, **Power**, turn on or off **Flip to start** option.

![Flip to start](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT517196/Flip to start_20260121072652196.jpg)



#### What should I do if my computer battery is discharging quickly?

If your computer battery is discharging quickly, try the following methods:

* Terminate any power-consuming settings, operations, or running apps, such as:  
  
  - Decrease the screen brightness.  
  
  - Turn off the keyboard backlight.  
  
  - Disconnect unnecessary peripheral devices.
* Ensure your computer is not set to high performance mode, which can consume more power. To change the settings:  
  
  1. Right-click the ![Quick settings area](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT516770/3-4-2 Quick settings area_20241029025512549.jpg) Battery icon in the quick settings area on the right side of the taskbar.  
  
  2. Click **Power and sleep settings**.  
  
  3. Locate the **Power mode** section and change the mode.
* Update the battery driver to the latest version. For details, refer to **How to update or install the latest device driver?** in this article.
* If your computer battery is old or drained, consider replacing the battery.


#### What should I do if the audio is not working?

If you encounter any audio problems, such as no audio or malfunctioning audio, you can try the following solutions to troubleshoot and fix the problems:

* Verify the audio settings:  
  
  1. Go to Windows **Settings** > **System** > **Sound**.  
  
  2. Verify that the sound **Output** and **Input** devices are selected correctly.   
  
  3. Verify that the **Volume** is properly set and is not muted.
* Run the audio troubleshooter:  
  
  1. Go to Windows **Settings** > **System** > **Sound**.  
  
  2. Find **Troubleshoot common sound problems** under **Advanced**. Click **Output devices** or **Input devices** to troubleshoot and fix the problem.

For more solutions to audio problems, refer to [Fix audio or sound problems in Windows 10 and 11](https://support.lenovo.com/solutions/ht501860).



#### Why is my keyboard backlight not working?

It could be caused by the following reasons:

* The keyboard backlight might be turned off or the backlight level might be low. To turn on or adjust the keyboard backlight, press fn + space key to toggle among on, brighter, off, and auto mode.
* The keyboard driver might be out-of-date or uninstalled. To update or install drivers, refer to **How to update or install the latest device driver?** in this article.


#### Why does my screen brightness change constantly?

Your computer may include an ambient light sensor with the adaptive brightness feature enabled. The light sensor can detect the intensity of the surrounding light. Using data provided by the sensor, the operating system can dynamically adjust the screen brightness. The adaptive brightness feature of the operating system can be disabled.

For Windows operating systems, adaptive brightness settings are usually found in Windows **Settings** > **System** > **Display**. For more information, see [Content adaptive brightness control in Windows](https://support.microsoft.com/windows/content-adaptive-brightness-control-in-windows-292d1f7f-9e02-4b37-a9c8-dab3e1727e78).



#### Why does my computer enter sleep mode suddenly when it is in use?

Your computer may include a hall sensor. The hall sensor can detect the strength and direction of a magnetic field produced by a permanent magnet or an electromagnet. It is used mainly for putting the computer into sleep mode when you close the lid.

![Hall sensor](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT517196/FAQ_Hall sensor_20250103030542583.png)

The area a indicates the location of the hall sensor. Keep anything that can produce a magnetic field from this area. Otherwise, your computer may enter sleep mode suddenly when you are using it.



#### Why does Copilot in Windows not open when I press the Copilot key on my keyboard?

It could be caused by the following reasons:

* Copilot in Windows is available in select global markets. Your region may not support Copilot. In such a case, it opens Windows Search instead of Copilot when pressing the ![Copilot Key Icon](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT516770/3-2-1 Copilot Key Icon_20241024090414199.png) key.
* Your Windows operating system version is not up to date and does not include the necessary software components. Update your Windows operating system to version 23H2 or later using Windows Update and then try again.  
  
  To check for updates, open **Start** > **Settings** > **Windows Update** , then click **Check for updates**. If updates are available, you can choose to install them.  
  **Note:** The Version 23H2 update may not be immediately available for your PC. You may need to periodically open Windows Update and manually check for updates to install the 23H2 update when it becomes available for your PC. For more information, see [Windows Update: FAQ](https://support.microsoft.com/windows/windows-update-faq-8a903416-6f45-0718-f5c7-375e92dddeb2#).
