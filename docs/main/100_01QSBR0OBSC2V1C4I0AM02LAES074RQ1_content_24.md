### Symptom

External Type-C monitor has a black screen during BIOS flash if connected to Type-C monitor and lid is closed.

### Applicable Brands

ThinkPad

### Applicable Systems

ThinkPad T14s Gen 6 (Type 21N1, 21N2)

### Operating Systems

Windows 11 ARM (24H2 or later)

### Limitation

Do not power off or reset the system. Wait until Lenovo Logo is displayed. This is the expected behavior and is not a an issue.
