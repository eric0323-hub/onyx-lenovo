### Description

When multiple graphic cards are installed and the system has 1TB or more of memory, the system may blue screen (BSOD) after updating the OS to version Win11 24H2 26100.7623 or Win11 25H2 26200.7623 when resuming from sleep or hibernate.

### Applicable Brands

ThinkStation

### Applicable Systems

ThinkStation PX

### System Is Configured With

* 1TB or more of memory
* 2 or more graphic cards
* Win11 OS (update MSFT 2026.1 KB or newer, OS version 26100.7623/26200.7623 or higher

### Operating Systems

* Win11 24H2 26100.7623 or higher
* Win11 25H2 26200.7623 or higher

### Workaround

Workaround 1: Uninstall all 2026.1 KB updates under Windows Update

### Uninstall MSFT KB

Workaround 2: Disable S3 and S4 and uncheck “Turn on fast startup" under OS setting
