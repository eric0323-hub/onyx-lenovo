### Symptom

When a ThinkPad system is placed on top of a closed ThinkPad, it may detect the underlying system's lid and perform the "When I close the lid" action. This may cause the LCD to turn off or the system to go into sleep/hibernation, depending on the system configuration.

For general display issues, see [Troubleshooting Display or Monitor Issues](https://support.lenovo.com/solutions/ht510324).

### Applicable Brands

ThinkPad

### Solution

Do not operate a ThinkPad while on top of another ThinkPad or other computing device.
