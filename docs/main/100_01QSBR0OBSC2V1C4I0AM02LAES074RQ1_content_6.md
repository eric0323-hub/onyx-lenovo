**STATEMENT DESCRIPTION:** 

How to keep the previous status of the computer after the lid being closed, besides entering to Standby or Hibernate mode?

**SOLUTION:** 

1. Go to Start à Control Panel à Power Options à Advanced.

2. Under Power buttons, select Standby under When I close the lid of my portable computer.
