## Symptom

The device produces an electrical tingle when touched during charging.

## Applicable systems

* IdeaPad Slim5 16IMH9 (83DC)
  
  Yoga Slim7 14IMH9 (83CV)

## Solution

If the customer has not yet done so, advise them to first review the following official Lenovo support article before requesting further assessment of their device:

[Information about the mild tingling sensation (https://support.lenovo.com/solutions/ht505170)](https://support.lenovo.com/solutions/ht505170)

As outlined in this article:

* The mild tingling sensation is caused by the AC power adapter, not the notebook itself.
* The adapter is CB-certified and has undergone testing for touch current/leakage current in compliance with the IEC 60950-1 safety standard.

If the customer remains unsatisfied with this explanation or continues to experience concerns, coordinate with local Lenovo support to replace the 3-prong AC power adapter.
