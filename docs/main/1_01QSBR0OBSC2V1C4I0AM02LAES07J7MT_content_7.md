## 📌 Known Issues with ThinkPad P1 Gen 6

## 🔄 Recovery Steps

To recover a system that is already in the failed state, please follow these steps:

1. **Remove the 48GB DIMM(s)**.
2. **Insert a lower capacity DIMM**.
3. **Power on the machine** and log into the operating system.
4. **Update the BIOS** to version **1.16 or higher**.
5. **Remove the lower capacity DIMM**.
6. **Reinstall the 48GB DIMM(s)**.

## ⚠️ Important Note

If a lower capacity DIMM is not available, please order one for follow-up repair and include these steps in the Work Order (WO) template.

For further assistance, please refer to the detailed solution guide: [CLICK HERE](https://pcsupport.lenovo.com/us/en/products/laptops-and-netbooks/thinkpad-p-series-laptops/thinkpad-p1-gen-6-type-21fv-21fw/21fv/21fvcto1ww/pw08zf8p/solutions/ht515923).
