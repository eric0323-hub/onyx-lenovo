### Description

Wi-Fi is not connecting properly or the signal drops intermittently. For additional troubleshooting information, see [Frequent drops or intermittent wireless connection - Windows 10](https://support.lenovo.com/solutions/ht502846).

### Applicable Brands

* ideapad
* ThinkPad

### Solution

Check for Wi-Fi driver updates and download the most recent. [Lenovo Vantage: Using your PC just got easier](https://support.lenovo.com/solutions/ht505081).

Use the following steps to download a driver:

1. Visit <https://support.lenovo.com>.
2. Choose your product.
3. Choose **Drivers & Software**.  
   ![Drivers and Software](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508661/driversandsoftware2024_20240626120453103.png)
4. Select **Automatic Update** or **Manual Update**.  
   ![Updates](https://download.lenovo.com/km/media/images/HT508661/automaticdriverupdateJan2023_20230126153157835.png)
5. For **Manual Update**, select **Networking: Wireless LAN** and download.  
   
   For **Automatic Update**, select **Scan Now** and follow the on-screen instructions.

For Wi-Fi troubleshooting information, see [Popular Topics: Wireless, Wi-Fi](https://support.lenovo.com/solutions/ht101634).
